## 4. NEWS PAGE (/news)

### 4.1 Page Title
- "News & Events"

### 4.2 News Items (Chronological, newest first)
Each news item should include:
- **Date** (e.g., "April 2025")
- **Separator** (e.g., " - ")
- **Headline** (bold)
- **Description** (optional, justify text)

#### Example News Categories:
- Awards and recognitions
- Paper publications/highlights
- Thesis defenses
- Scholarship winners

## 5. PUBLICATIONS PAGE (/publications)

### 5.1 Google Scholar Link
- Link to group's Google Scholar profile (at top of page)

### 5.2 In Preparation Section
- **Section Heading:** "In preparation"
- Papers currently being prepared:
  - Format: Authors. Title, in preparation (Year).

### 5.3 Published Papers by Year
Organized chronologically (newest first), for each year:

#### Year 202X
- **Year Heading**
- List of papers with format:
  - **Authors.** Title. *Journal* Volume, [Pages/Article Number] (Year).
  - DOI link (optional but recommended)

#### Formatting Notes:
- Journal names should be italicized
- Page numbers can be hyperlinked to DOI
- Continue for each year (2025, 2024, 2023, 2022, 2021, etc.)
